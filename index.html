About Us

Welcome to Thai Lagoon, where the flavors of Thailand come alive. Family-owned and operated, we are passionate about sharing authentic Thai cuisine made from fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and a touch of modern flair. Whether you're craving classic favorites like Pad Thai and Green Curry or trying our specials, we are the perfect place to enjoy a memorable meal with friends and family. Join us at Thai Lagoon and experience the wonderful flavors of Thai cuisine right in your neighborhood! We are excited to have you!
